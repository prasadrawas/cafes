Our Story

A Hidden Gem That
Blends Style & Flavor

Nestled on the first floor of CK Tower in Dashmesh Nagar, Chéri Cafe & Eatery is where elegance meets comfort. Our thoughtfully curated decor creates a warm, inviting atmosphere — whether you're here for a coffee date, a casual dinner, a solo work session, or a gathering with friends.

We believe every dish should be a celebration. From our signature continental plates to beloved North Indian classics, each creation is bursting with flavor and beautifully presented. Our top-notch coffee and irresistible desserts keep guests coming back.

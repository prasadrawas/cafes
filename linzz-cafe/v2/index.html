Our Story

Where Every Sip
Tells a Story

Nestled in the heart of Town Center at Connaught Place, Linzz Café is where artistry meets flavor.

With its warm minimalist interiors, marble-top tables, stunning glass ceiling, and Instagram-worthy decor, every visit feels like an experience. From the town's first Chocolate Fondue to expertly crafted latte art, we celebrate the joy of great food and beautiful spaces.
